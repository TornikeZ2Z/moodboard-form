The Studio

Calm, character and craft — designed for the way you actually live.

Espacio is a Tbilisi-based architecture and interior design practice led by two architects. We work across residential and commercial projects — private homes and apartments, retail spaces, cafés and bakeries, offices and clinics.

Every project starts with how a space will be used, then layers material, light and detail until it feels considered but never cold. We stay involved from concept and visualisation through to the finishes installed on site.
